This repository contains a collection of end-to-end Machine Learning business case studies developed during my ML Coding, Computer Vision, & NLP certification through Scaler. 

The goal of these projects is to apply advanced ML concepts (Supervised/Unsupervised Learning, NLP, and Neural Networks) to solve practical, real-world business problems.

## 📂 Projects Included

* **E-commerce Product Recommendation System:** Build and evaluate a hybrid recommendation engine (content-based+ collaborative filtering) for an e-commerce platform using product metadata and
user behavior data.
* **E-commerce Marketing & Sales Solution:** The e-commerce company aims to leverage data-driven insights to enhance customer acquisition, retention, and revenue optimization. 
* **Predicting Sales from Campaign Data:** Marketing teams often invest heavily in influencer campaigns — paying creators to promote products on social media platforms like Instagram. But understanding which factors actually drive product sales is critical to making better decisions about who to work with, how much to spend, and how to design content.The goal is to build a predictive model that can estimate how many units of a product will be sold based on key influencer campaign attributes.
* **Book Price Prediction:** Build the system to predict the book price based on historical data related to author, sales, topics etc.
* **IPL Data Analysis Case Study:** The franchise wants data-driven decision-making across match strategy, player selection, venue planning, and fan engagement. You’ve been given the official IPL dataset (matches, seasons, venues, outcomes, basic match-level metadata) covering 2008–2024.Your task is to analyze the dataset, build predictive and prescriptive models, and deliver clear, actionable recommendations that the team’s Head of Cricket Operations can use before a season. Submit a single Jupyter Notebook that contains the code, visualizations, model outputs, and an executive summary with prioritized business recommendations and assumptions.
